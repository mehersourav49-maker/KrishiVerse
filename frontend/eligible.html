Eligible Schemes for You

CM-Kisan Scheme

Direct cash support to small, marginal, and landless farmers of Odisha. Recently disbursed ₹1,041 crore to 51 lakh farmers for Rabi season.

Samruddha Krushak Yojana

Ensures minimum procurement price of ₹3,100 per quintal for paddy. Helps boost farmer income with guaranteed MSP support.

CM Agricultural Entrepreneurship Scheme

Offers subsidies (up to ₹1 crore) for young agri-entrepreneurs in storage, cold chains, and processing infrastructure.

Support for Landless Families

Landless households receive ₹12,500 for allied activities such as goat farming, poultry, mushroom, and beekeeping.

Mukhyamantri Kamadhenu Yojana

Distribution of 10,000 high-yielding cows and investment in biogas plants to boost dairy production in Odisha.

Coffee Plantation Expansion

Expanding coffee cultivation into Rayagada, Keonjhar, and Mayurbhanj districts to benefit tribal and small farmers.

PM-Kisan Samman Nidhi

Provides ₹6,000 per year in 3 installments to small and marginal farmers across India via direct bank transfer.

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

Crop insurance scheme covering crop loss due to droughts, floods, pests, or diseases at subsidized premium rates.

Agriculture Infrastructure Fund (AIF)

₹1 lakh crore fund for cold storage, warehouses, food processing, and supply chain infrastructure.

Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs)

Support for 10,000 FPOs with financial aid, training, equity grants, and market access via eNAM.

Kisan Credit Card (KCC)

Affordable short-term credit up to ₹5 lakh with interest subvention. Prompt repayment reduces interest to 4%.

PM-Kisan Maandhan Yojana

Pension scheme: farmers contribute ₹55–₹200/month and get ₹3,000/month after 60 years. Govt matches contribution.

PM-KUSUM Scheme

Provides 60% subsidy for solar irrigation pumps and allows farmers to sell surplus solar power to the grid.

PM Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY)

Fisheries development scheme supporting ponds, hatcheries, fish markets, and cold storage with subsidies.

Soil Health Card Scheme

Provides farmers with soil test-based reports and advice for balanced fertilization and better productivity.

PM Krishi Sinchai Yojana (PMKSY)

Ensures efficient water use with irrigation projects and micro-irrigation to increase farm productivity.

Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY)

Promotes organic farming by developing clusters and providing financial assistance to organic farmers.

National Beekeeping & Honey Mission (NBHM)

Encourages beekeeping with infrastructure support, FPO integration, and honey production incentives.

Dhan-Dhaanya Krishi Yojana (PMDDKY)

New central scheme for 100 agriculture districts integrating 36 schemes across 11 ministries.
