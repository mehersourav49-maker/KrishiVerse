IoT Drip Irrigation System

Automated irrigation for maximum water efficiency

About the Device

The IoT Drip Irrigation System combines drip irrigation with smart sensors and controllers. It automatically waters crops based on soil moisture, weather data, and crop type.

Uses

  • Automated watering based on soil moisture levels
  • Remote control of irrigation through mobile app
  • Supports multiple crop zones in one farm
  • Integrates with weather forecasts for water-saving

Installation Guide

  1. Lay out drip pipes across the field according to crop rows.
  2. Connect soil moisture sensors near the crop root zone.
  3. Install a central controller with Wi-Fi or GSM connectivity.
  4. Link the system to a mobile app for monitoring and scheduling.

Benefits

  • Up to 50% water savings compared to traditional irrigation
  • Reduces labor cost by automating watering tasks
  • Ensures crops get the right water at the right time
  • Boosts crop quality and yield
