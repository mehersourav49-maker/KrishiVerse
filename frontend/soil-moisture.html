About the Device

The Smart Soil Moisture Sensor is an IoT-enabled device that measures the volumetric water content of soil in real time. It connects to mobile apps and dashboards, allowing farmers to make informed irrigation decisions. This sensor reduces water wastage, optimizes crop growth, and supports sustainable farming practices.

Uses

  • Real-time soil moisture monitoring
  • Integration with smart irrigation systems
  • Prevent overwatering and underwatering
  • Improve water efficiency in agriculture
  • Use in research, greenhouses, and large farms

Installation Guide

  1. Select the target area (farm, garden, or greenhouse).
  2. Insert the probe vertically into soil at root depth.
  3. Power the device using batteries or solar module.
  4. Connect it to Wi-Fi or Bluetooth through the mobile app.
  5. Sync the device ID in your app dashboard.
Tip: Place multiple sensors in large fields for more accurate soil coverage.
