Accurate weather data directly from your farm

About the Device

The Portable Weather Station is a compact device that provides real-time farm-level weather data including temperature, rainfall, humidity, and wind speed. Farmers can use it for better planning and decision-making.

Uses

  • Monitor local temperature and humidity
  • Measure rainfall and forecast irrigation needs
  • Track wind speed for pesticide spraying safety
  • Integrate with crop advisory systems

Installation Guide

  1. Place the station in an open field without shade or obstruction.
  2. Mount it on a stand or pole about 2 meters above the ground.
  3. Connect to solar or battery power supply.
  4. Sync data with the mobile app or web dashboard.

Benefits

  • Farm-level weather accuracy better than city forecasts
  • Helps optimize irrigation and fertilizer scheduling
  • Improves crop disease and pest management
  • Portable and easy to install anywhere
